About me!

I am an ambitious and energetic student, who commits most of his time, to learning new upcoming technologies. I am proficient with English, Spanish, Hindi and Marathi languages. I also have good experience with hardware microcontrollers like Arduino, Mbed and also the Raspberry pi having done various Internet of Things projects. I am skilled at competitive programming as a virtue of being the operations manager of SIESGSTarena, a CODECHEF student chapter and tech head the IEEE student chapter of SIES Graduate School of Technology. I have also worked with 2D and 3D graphic animation and editing giving me valuable experience in the Adobe master collection, CorelDraw, 3D studio max. I was introduced to machine learning as an undergraduate, and have dedicated most of my time to mastering this field ever since.
